## Day 1 — Sunday, May 3, 2026
**Route:** Palm Springs → Amboy → Kingman → Flagstaff  
**Drive time:** ~7 hrs  
**Start:** Palm Springs, CA

**Opening note:**
Early, dramatic departure. Sunglasses on. Coffee in hand. No one speaks for the first 30 minutes.

### Stops
- **Amboy, CA — Roy’s Motel & Cafe**  
  Post-apocalyptic chic. You will feel something.
- **Oatman, AZ**  
  Wild burros, Old West chaos, zero dignity. They *will* judge you.
- **Cool Springs Station**  
  Desert gas station fantasy. Photos required. Hydration mandatory.
- **Hackberry General Store**  
  Absolute Route 66 shrine. Americana maximalism. Time loses meaning.
- **Peach Springs, AZ**  
  Quiet, scenic stretch of original road. A brief emotional reset.
- **Seligman (drive-through)**  
  Birthplace of Route 66 revival. Honk, wave, keep moving.
- **Williams, AZ**  
  Neon diners, souvenir overload, train nostalgia. Lean in.

**End:** Flagstaff, AZ

### Sleep
- **First Choice:** Americana Motor Hotel
- **Second Choice:** Little America Flagstaff

**Vibe:**
First-day excitement. Everyone is helpful. Everyone is wrong about directions. No arguments yet.

**Authorized behaviors for Day 1:**
Talking about how “this isn’t even that far,” playlist micromanaging, outfit planning out loud.
